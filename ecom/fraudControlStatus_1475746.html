Resurs Bank e-Commerce Platform : fraudControlStatus

Created by Benny, last modified on 2017-02-27

The status of the payment fraud control.

ValueDescription
FROZENThe payment is currently frozen. This typically means that there is something that needs further investigation before the payment can be finalized.
BOOKEDThe payment is not frozen, and may be finalized at any time.
CONTROL_IN_PROGRESS

The deprecated flow: the automatic fraud control is still in progress. It will result in FROZEN or BOOKED.

In the simplified flow you will get an intermediate status of FROZEN when the control is still running.
